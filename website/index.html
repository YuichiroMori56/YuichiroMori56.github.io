Yuichiro Mori

MD, MPH, PhD

  • Researcher, Department of Health Promotion and Behavioral Sciences, Kyoto University Graduate School of Medicine
  • L-INSIGHT Fellow, Kyoto University

I am a board-certified cardiologist and interventional cardiologist, currently a researcher at Kyoto University's School of Public Health. After clinical training in cardiology and catheter interventions, I pursued an MPH at Johns Hopkins and a PhD at Kyoto University, motivated by the conviction that much of the cardiovascular disease burden is rooted in social and structural determinants. My research combines epidemiology, causal inference, and implementation science to advance the prevention of cardio-kidney-metabolic disease — aiming for a society where people are naturally guided toward healthier choices before disease onset.

Research Interests

  • Cardiovascular-Kidney-Metabolic Syndrome
  • Causal Inference
  • Target Trial Emulation
  • Machine Learning in Epidemiology
  • Preventive Cardiology
  • Health Screening & Policy
  • Real-World Evidence
